Master of Education and Postgraduate Certificate in Education

This program combines a solid foundation in education theory and the flexibility to pursue your own interests through elective subjects.

Course structure

There are three options of study.

  • Postgraduate Certificate in Education- 50 points of any subject from a specified list (subjects may be 12.5 or 25 points). Detailed course and subject information is available in the University handbook.
  • Master of Education Coursework-3 core subjects, 5 electives. Detailed course and subject information is available in the University handbook.
  • Master of Education Research- 2 core subjects, 2 subjects from an elective theme, 20,000 word major thesis & research methods module. You will complete the coursework subjects before starting your thesis. We also offer a Master of Philosophy for students interested in pursuing a research masters. Detailed course and subject information is available in the University handbook.

Elective themes

Master of Education students can choose their electives from our specialist masters courses or from the following themes:

  • Arts Education
  • Curriculum and Pedagogy
  • Digital Technologies in Education
  • Educational Management and Leadership
  • Equity, Youth and Identity
  • Language and Literacy Education
  • Mathematics and Numeracy Education
  • Science Education
  • Thinking, Knowledge and Creativity
  • Teaching Shakespeare

At a glance

Entry requirements:

An undergraduate degree and a fourth-year level teaching qualification, or equivalent
or a four-year teaching degree, or equivalent

Research students must maintain a grade average of 75% in all coursework to progress to thesis

All applicants must also meet English language requirements

Duration:

Postgraduate Certificate: 6 months full-time/1 year part-time

Masters- Coursework: 1 year full-time/ 2 years part-time

Masters- Research: 1.5 years full-time, 3 years part-time

Fees:

Postgraduate certificate: Australian fee place
Master- coursework: CSP, Australian fee place
Master- research: Research training scheme place, Australian fee place
