E4Kids Mailing List

E4Kids-refgroup is an email alert service for the E4Kids (Effective Early Educational Experiences) study.
This is a low volume email list which will keep you up to date with developments in the project, such as new publications, conference announcements, position vacancies, research findings and other issues.
Anyone with an interest in E4Kids is invited to subscribe.

Please contact e4-kids@unimelb.edu.au should you have any questions about E4Kids mailing list.
