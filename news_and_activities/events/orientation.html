2014 Welcome and Orientation

Orientation is designed to welcome you to the Melbourne Graduate School of Education and help you get the best out of your student experience. You will meet our academics and professional support staff and learn about the services available to assist and support you. We encourage you to also participate in the central university activities.

For more information on central university orientation activities, please go to Graduate Orientation and the Melbourne School of Graduate Research.

If you are an international student, visit Student Support-International Students for helpful information and support.
