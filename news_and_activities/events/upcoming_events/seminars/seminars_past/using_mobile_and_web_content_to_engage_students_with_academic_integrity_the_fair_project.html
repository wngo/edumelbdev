Using Mobile and Web Content to Engage Students with Academic Integrity: The FAIR Project

Date: Wednesday 10, April 2013 
Time: 1:00pm- 2:00pm
Venue: Barbara Falk room, CSHE, Lvl 1, 715 Swanston St.
RSVP: Visit the Innovations in Teaching and Learning seminar series website for seminar information and registration

Recent changes in higher education have highlighted the need to engage university students in the process of “learning how to learn”, and as a result, academics are increasingly being asked to teach academic skills and integrity alongside disciplinary knowledge. However, in addressing this task, we are faced with a serious challenge: how can we make the learning of what is often perceived as technical and even dry content more relevant and engaging for our students?

This talk will showcase a recent innovative approach to fostering students’ understanding of academic integrity - ‘FAIR: From Academic Integrity to Responsibility’ - that makes use of mobile and web content development platforms to deliver a timely, interactive, and personalised learning experience to students.

This seminar is part of the Innovations in Teaching and Learning seminar series.
