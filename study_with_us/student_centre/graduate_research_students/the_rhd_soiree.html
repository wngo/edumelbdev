The RHD Soirée

Most months we hold an event that brings graduate research higher degree (RHD) students together. This includes research orientation events, the Graduate Research Conferences and the RHD Soirée.

The RHD Soirée is a gathering that brings together RHD students over fine food and beverages and they focus on a particular activity or initiative. We aim to evoke a sense of the luxury, comfort and refinement associated with the traditional soirée at these late afternoon events!

We encourage research students to come up with ideas and initiatives for events. Please send through any suggestions to mgse-gradresearchevents@lists.unimelb.edu.au

The University’s 3MT competition is a vibrant annual event that provides a vehicle for RHD students to communicate their research interest to an interested audience in the space of 3 minutes.

At this gala RHD Soirée event, MSGE research students who have already elected to take part in the university’s 3MT competition will have an in-house opportunity to present their 3-minute thesis oration at the MGSE, prior to taking part in their University heat. Special guests at the Soirée will include MGSE alumni, Melbourne School of Graduate Research and MGSE staff. Last year, Melinda Kong and Cui Xia gave presentations at one of our Soirées, where they received constructive criticism before giving their excellent presentations in the university heats, with Melinda making it through to the Grand Final. A number of students who came to the Soirée were inspired and excited by the idea of entering the Competition this year. So, here is your chance!

Support and advice on how to get started is available from the MSGR Academic Support Team. The University 3MT Competition will be calling for participants at the end of May.

If you are interested in taking part in the MGSE Pre-3MT RHD Soirée, can you please let us know at mgse-gradresearchevents@lists.unimelb.edu.au
