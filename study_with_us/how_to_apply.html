How to Apply

Apply for the Master of Teaching

Domestic students

Applications for the 2014 intake of Master of Teaching have now closed. We are not accepting late applications.

Our next intake for this program will be in 2015. Applications will open in August through the Victorian Tertiary Admissions Centre (VTAC).

All applicants are assessed on their previous academic record and their responses to TeacherSelector, an online tool that helps us determine whether you have the right skills for the Master of Teaching. We use it to assess applications because the Master of Teaching is a challenging course and we are seeking students who are most likely to succeed.
  

International students

Applications for the 2014 intake of the Master of Teaching have now closed. We are not accepting late applications.

Our next intake for this program will be in 2015. We will commence accepting applications shortly.

Apply for a coursework program

Domestic students

Applications for all graduate coursework programs have now closed. We may accept late applications for some courses** subject to availability. Apply via the Online Application System.

To apply for subject credit please submit the credit application form with your course application. Please refer to the Credit Policy before submitting your application.


**excludes Master of Applied Positive Psychology, Master of Educational Psychology and Master of Teaching.

International students

Applications for all graduate coursework programs have now closed. We may accept late applications for some courses** subject to availability. Visit the Future Students website for more information on applying for a coursework program.

**excludes Master of Applied Positive Psychology, Master of Educational Psychology and Master of Teaching. 

Apply for a research program

Application form

Graduate Research Admissions

How to complete your application

Applying for a research degree is a comprehensive process. Refer to our Guidance for RHD applicants to assist in preparing your application. Please note that incomplete applications may not be accepted.

If you have any questions about how to complete or submit your application, please contact our Future Students team.

Application closing dates

New applications may be submitted throughout the year with application closing dates for each semester.

Semester 2, 2014
  • International students: 30 April 2014
  • Domestic students: 30 May 2014
Semester 1, 2015
  • International students: 31 October 2014
  • Domestic students: 28 November 2014*

*Note the scholarship deadline is 31 October 2014

Scholarships

Candidates wishing to be considered for a scholarship must submit a completed application (including all supporting documentation) by 31 October 2014 in order to be considered for the 2015 scholarship rounds. Visit Melbourne Scholarships to learn more.

What happens next?

You will receive an email to confirm receipt of your application within five business days. This email will let you know whether your application is complete or if further documents are required.

Your application will then be assessed by our selection committee. If you have applied for a scholarship, your application will be forwarded to the scholarship committee.

When can I expect to be notified of the outcome of my application?

The application assessment process usually takes eight weeks from the date of the email receipt of your complete application. All correspondence, including notification of the outcome will be by email.

If successful you will receive an offer letter, explaining how you can accept your place.

Apply for a breadth subject

Speak to a Student Adviser in the faculty you are currently enrolled with to apply for a breadth subject.

*Domestic students include Australian citizens, Australian permanent resident and New Zealand citizens.
