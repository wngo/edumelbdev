How can we help you?

We can:

  • set you on the right path to choosing teaching as a career or taking your next step in education study
  • guide you through the choices available when enrolling in a program (e.g. learning areas, choosing subjects to suit your interests and balancing coursework and research options)
  • deliver workshops to refresh your academic skills, in partnership with Academic Skills
  • help with enrolment, assignment submission and changing your personal details
  • advise on applying for credit, withdrawing from a program, taking a leave of absence or deferring
  • provide information about enrichment opportunities such as mentoring, study abroad, exchange and volunteering
  • provide information on research candidature and thesis examination
  • help you find University support services such as financial assistance, disability liaison or counselling.

Give us a call or pop in to the Student Centre to find out more. If your enquiry is detailed, call us to make an appointment with a student adviser.
